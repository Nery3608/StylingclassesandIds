1 pound of chicken breast
    2 tablespoons of Paprika
    3 tablespoons of Salt
    4 tablespoons of Pepper
    2 cups of Buttermilk
    1 teaspoon of Garlic
    Slap the chicken breast with paprika and salt
    Rub the Pepper into the meat
    Put the buttermilk in a bowl
    Pour the Garlic into the bowl
    Let the Chicken marinate in the buttermilk

The color may be worrying, that's okay!

Don't worry about swapping the seasonings for something else!
